SF2000 Silent Sounds Pack

These are replacement sound effects for the SF2000 retro handheld to make the sound effects silent.

To use, add these files to the /Resources folder on your microSD card.

Complete Pack:

SF2000_Silent_Sounds_Pack.zip: Contains all sound files listed below (Except Background Music, see below)

Individual Sound Files:

  • c2fkec.pgt: "Popping" sound that is played when moving around the search keyboard
  • mfsvr.nkf: "Beep" sound that is played when entering a letter on the search keyboard
  • nyquest.gdb: "Whooshing" sound that is played when switching between emulated systems on the main menu, between the "Resume", "Quit", "Load" and "Save" options on the in-game menu, and scrolling by pages within a system's game-list
  • oldversion.kbe: "Three Note Upward Chime" sound that is played when deleting a letter on the search keyboard
  • swapfile.sys: "Squishy" sound played when navigating horizontally through "shortcut" games on the main menu, or vertically within a system's game-list

Unused sounds (included for completeness sake):

  • dpnet.dll: "Downwards Zap" sound, unknown usage
  • dsreg.bvs: "Upwards Bleeping" sound, unknown usage
  • help.lis: "Double Buzzer" sound, unknown usage

Background Music:

pagefile.sys: Silent main menu background music (credit to VonMillhausen)

Kerokero - SF2000 BGM Tool

Kerokero is a background music creator for the Data Frog SF2000 console. It supports .WAV and .MP3 formats, allowing users to preview, mark in/out points, adjust gain, and save clips in various formats including a specialized 'pagefile.sys' format.
